Children's Unit

The OAMC Pediatrics unit was established for the special needs of children in the Greater Montreal area and surroundings. Equipped with the latest medical technologies, and staffed by empathic healthcare professionals of the highest quality, the OAMC Pediatrics unit provides children with the care they deserve. This child friendly environment is the right alternative to more stressful adult hospital environments. It features colorful paint, a modern play area that is constantly supervised, and frequent activities and visits from entertainers and volunteers.
