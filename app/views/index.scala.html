Welcome to the Open Access Medical Center!

The OAMC is Montreal's foremost medical care provider, with three specialized units covering all patient needs. The surgical unit, palliative care unit and children's unit are located on a beautiful urban campus in the heart of Downtown Montreal, with direct access to the metro green line and the nearby highway 67. The OAMC has deep historic roots in the local Universities, and draws most of its staff from them and the local community, as well as some of the finest professionals from around the world. It is a bilingual hospital providing full service in both official languages, and stresses the provision of empathic, professional, and cutting-edge healthcare to the greater community. The OAMC exists to make the health of Montrealers, patients, and employees the best it can be.
