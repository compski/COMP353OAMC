Palliative Care Unit

The OAMC palliative care unit is devoted to providing the best medical care for patients suffering from serious, long-term illness. Our staff of nurses, doctors, and allied health professionals are all highly trained in the latest techniques and technologies for the relief of physical and emotional pain. The modern facilities provide a restful place for patients to enjoy the highest quality of life with all their physical, emotional, social, and spiritual needs met and easy access to their friends and loved ones.
